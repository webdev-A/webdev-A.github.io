Welcome To Al Jograts Persian Grill Little Story

Al Jograts started as a passion project when owners could not find an authentic Persian restaurant to satisfy their taste buds. Having found the chef who used to cook for their now defunct go-to Persian restaurant, they decided it was the perfect time to put up a business with his help. What sets Al Jograts apart from the competition is that it is driven by passion, as the owners themselves are avid enthusiasts of Persian food, wishing to share their vision with food lovers around the metro.

Food Guidelines

Hot Items

For Beef/Chicken Kebab, Beef/Chicken Shawarma, Chili Beef, Keema:

Heat in the microwave for up to 2 mins before serving.
Best consume within 5 days if stored in Ref/Chiller
and 10 days if stored in Freezer.

For Curry Sauce:

Shake bottle before heating. Heat in microwave for 1 minute before serving.
Best consume within 10days. Store in Ref only. Do not store in freezer.

For Pita:

This can last up to 30days as long as stored in the freezer.
You can heat in the microwave or toast in pan.

For Basmati/Biryani Rice:

Heat in the microwave for up to 2mins before serving.
Best consume within 7 days.

Cold Items

For Garlic/Chili Garlic/Garlic Herb Sauce:

Shake before use. Best consumed within 10days.
Store in Ref Only. Do not store in freezer.

For Hummus:

Always refrigerate. Pls consume within 3-5days.

If you do not have microwave at home you can use to your pans instead.

Please note that we do not use any kind of preservative
so we recommend to consume everything within our recommended time.
